My Projects

Here is the biggest projects I have made during my studies.


VR Game - Prison Breakout VR

Our VR game is a jailbreak escape room. The aim of the game is to complete puzzles and escape the prison in less than 15 minutes. The puzzles have the right amount of difficulty and variety to make the game suitable for everyone.

We made this game in a three person group. I was in charge of designing and doing almost the entire map, and the win/lose screen. The game was presented in the Vectorama event in Oulu, where people could come and try the game out.

3D Game - Gun Guy Zombies

Gun Guy Zombies is a 3D horror first person shooter. The player has to go through a forest full of zombies while following a path to the end. We were making this game as a three person group. I was in charge of designing and doing the entire map and some small details.


2D Game - Gun Guy RPG 

Gun Guy RPG is an top down rpg style game, where the player goes trough the island, fighting zombies and enemies in a turn based combat style. The player can find money and spend it in stores to buy better equipment.   


Unreal Engine Game - PaintBall!

PaintBall! is a 5 man made multiplayer fps game, where the players fight eachother with paintballguns in different gamemodes and team sizes. We had a preview alpha version of the game in the Vectorama event.
